# Sushi Party — Support

Sushi Party is a scoreboard for all-you-can-eat sushi: tap to count plates, submit your tally, and track your eating history with friends.

## Contact us

Questions, bugs, or feedback? Email **[contact@joseppy.ca](mailto:contact@joseppy.ca)** and we'll get back to you.

## Common help

- **Counting:** Start a party, pick a restaurant (optional), then tap **+** on each item. Submit when you're done.
- **Location:** Optional — only used to find nearby restaurants. The app works fully without it.
- **Account & sign-in:** Sign in with email, Google, or Apple.
- **Delete your account:** See our [account deletion page](https://sushi-party-api.joseppy-workers.workers.dev/delete-account).
